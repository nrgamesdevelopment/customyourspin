1) What is Custom Your Spin?

Custom Your Spin is a lightning‑fast, free, and mobile‑friendly spin wheel generator and random name picker. It helps creators, teachers, event hosts, marketers and teams make fun, fair decisions in seconds. Our wheel spinner online runs entirely in your browser—no registration, no downloads, no limits.

Build a custom wheel maker for giveaway wheels, prize wheel games, decision maker wheel choices, classroom spinner tool activities, and viral Instagram Reels wheel challenges or YouTube Shorts. Choose polished wheel themes such as Classic wheel spinner, Pizza slice wheel, Slot machine, Poker chips wheel, or the special Flip Coin.

2) How to spin?

  1. Select a wheel theme from the “Wheel Theme” dropdown (Classic, Pizza, Slot Machine, Poker Chips, Flip Coin).
  2. Add entries in the Add Names box or use a Quick Template: Truth or Dare, This or That, Workout Challenge, Numbers 1‑10, Alphabet A‑Z, Colors Spin, Yes or No.
  3. Press Spin Wheel to start the animation (or Flip Coin for coin theme). A truly random winner is chosen.
  4. Use Remove Winner to keep picks unique and fair; click Reset All to clear everything.

Tip: Our adaptive text sizing ensures long names fit the slice. The engine behaves like a fair random picker tool suitable for classrooms, parties, streams and business giveaways.

5) What is a Quick Template?

Quick Templates are one‑click presets that fill the wheel with ready‑made lists so you can spin faster. Current presets include Truth or Dare, This or That, Workout Challenge, Numbers 1‑10, Alphabet A‑Z, Colors Spin, and Yes or No.

They are perfect for creators who want a start‑ready random picker, teachers running a classroom spinner tool, or anyone who needs a quick decision maker wheel.

6) How to add or remove names?

  • Type an entry in the Add Names box and click Add Name. You can add up to 50 names.
  • Use the Names on Wheel panel to review items. Click the delete icon to remove any name.
  • After each spin, press Remove Winner to eliminate the selected result from future spins.
  • Want a clean slate? Click Reset All to clear the list and results.

Pro tip: Combine Quick Templates with your own options to build powerful lists for giveaways, classroom picks, and viral wheel challenges.
